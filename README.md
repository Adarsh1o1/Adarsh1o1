### I'm Adarsh

I build things to understand how they work — not to have something to show.

Most of my projects started with a question I couldn't stop thinking about:

- How do files and folders actually work in the cloud? → built a Google Drive-like system from scratch
- How does real-time data move between devices over a network? → built a WebRTC app to use my phone as a 1080p webcam — shipped to Play Store, runs at ~20fps, and I know exactly why it's not 30
- Why is finding the right project partner in college so hard? → built a cross-college team-finding platform, owned backend and ops

The same curiosity carries into my YouTube channel (@adarshh1o1.me) — I explain how the internet actually works. DNS, CDNs, proxies, WebRTC. If I can't explain it clearly, I don't understand it yet.

Stack I reach for: Node.js · Django · WebRTC · WebSockets · DevOps
What I actually care about: why systems are designed the way they are — and what breaks when they're not.
